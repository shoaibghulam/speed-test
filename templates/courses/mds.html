Course Name : MDS (Master of Dental Surgery)

Eligibility :BDS recognized by DCI and completed one year internship Admission is through a competitive Examination PG NEET

Admission Process : Admission is made through counselling based on merit of National eligibility cum Entrance Test (PG NEET). Admission is made only in 1st year and last date for admission is 30th May of the admission year or as notified by NMC or appropriate Authority.

Hostel : Compulsory

Tuition Fee : Check Now , Other charges shall be extra

Examination System : Yearly

Regulation Body : DCI (Dental Council of India)

How to Apply : Candidate shall apply for PG-NEET in accordance to notification made on NEET website during Nov/ Dec once in a year. A candidate has to apply online to participate in NEET and will appear in examination online as per program notified on site. A candidate who will complete the rotational internship before 31st March can apply for NEET counselling. Qualified students have to participate on line state counselling during March / April. Candidate allotted seat shall report for admission at college on or before last date mentioned in the allotment letter along with fee and original documents.
