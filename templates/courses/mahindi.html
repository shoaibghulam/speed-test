Course Name : MA (Hindi)

Course Duration : 2 Years

Annual Intake : 30

Eligibility : Passed graduation (Hindi as one subject) from any recognized university with minimum 50% marks. In case of candidates belonging to SC/ ST must have obtained 45% instead 50%.

Admission Process : Admission is made only in 1st year and is made once in a year. The candidate, who fulfills the eligibility criteria, shall get registered. The candidate is required to appear in the written test on the notified date. The merit list shall be prepared on the basis of test and the admission list will be notified thereafter.

Hostel : Hostel charges (annual) shall be applicable as per the category of the hostel opted. However, cooperative mess charges shall be payable as per the provision of the mess.

Tuition Fee : Check Now , Other charges shall be extra

Examination System : Semester

How to Apply : The candidate seeking admission shall contact the admission cell and get registered with the university on the prescribed form. All the registered candidates shall appear in the written admission test on the date assigned and collect information for the admission test. The candidate, as per admission list, shall report for admission.
