Overview & Identity

Bareilly International University, Bareilly is established in accordance to UGC Act 1956 by the Uttar Pradesh Govt. Act No. 26 of 2016 dated 16th Sept 2016. The University is promoted by Rohilkhand Charitable Educational Trust and is functional from session 2016-17 with various courses.Our endeavor is to impart knowledge and develop skill to attain success by its pupil in professional and personal life by providing growing learning and research environment with due support of world class faculties, infrastructure, facilities and technology.
