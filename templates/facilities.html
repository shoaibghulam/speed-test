Campus Facilities

Bareilly International University believes in providing the best college experience to the students. With state of the art facilities and upgraded infrastructure BIU University is one of the best university in the State matching international standards.

Wi-Fi Campus:

In the fast expending global environment, it has become mandatory to remain in aware of technological and professional development in all segment of education. To meet this challenge the University campus made Wi Fi. Student are provided excess to it and in addition each department have been equipped with computer facilities with LAN. This facility help student in better learning, completing assignments and research. Additionally special and specific soft wares are also available for research students.

Library :

The central library is well equipped with growing collection of books, periodicals and journals function as learning resources and capable to caters the need of all the faculty.

  • Reading room facility.
  • National & International database.
  • Book lending services.
  • Reference & information services.
  • E-Library.
  • Free Internet services with medlars facilities.
    • In addition, departmental libraries are also functional. The library provides the following student support services.

    Auditorium:

    An air-cooled auditorium fully equipped having 900 seating capacity is available and the other air conditioned auditorium with seating capacity of above 400 and 200 each delegates are also available. The auditoriums are well equipped with advanced gadgets and suitable to organized national and international level academic events.

    Student Health Services:

    A fully equipped hospital with OPD and IPD services in all specialities take care of all health problems of all student and its habitants. The services are provided to students on concessional rates.

    Guest House:

    A well- built guest house providing delighted services is maintained in the campus. It has 27 AC rooms with dish TV. This facility is open to all type of University guest including parents of students.

    Cafeteria:

    A break in between hectic studies helps in re-energizing and strengthen learning process. With these ideology, spacious well-built cafeterias are in place where edibles with good nutrients are prepared and served in very hygienic environment. Separate cafeteria for students & staff are available in the campus

    Play Area:

    To keep students energetic and healthy, facilities for Indoor, Outdoor games, Gymnasium, yoga centre are made available. Sports facilities for Cricket, Badminton, Table Tennis, Volley Ball, Foot Ball, Basket Ball and Yoga Classes are available to all students inside the campus for maintaining physical fitness. Students are encouraged to participate in various events and Inter-Institutional tournaments. For cultural activities auditorium and stadium is also available

    Banking Facility:

    A branch of Central Bank of India is situated in the campus. Two Automated Teller Machines (ATM) are also available in the campus.
